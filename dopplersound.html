How it works

Sound waves travel at a constant speed (Mach 1.0).

  • Source moves right: Waves bunch up ahead (higher pitch).
  • Source moves left: Waves spread out behind (lower pitch).
  • Mach ≥ 1: Sonic boom (Mach cone) forms.
