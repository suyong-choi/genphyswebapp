The Condition

For a wheel of radius $R = 0.5\text{m}$ rolling without slipping, $v_{cm} = \omega R$. For example, if $v = 1.0\text{ m/s}$, then $\omega = 2.0\text{ rad/s}$.

Superposition

The red trace (Point P) moves at $2v_{cm}$ at the peak and $0$ at the contact point during pure rolling.

Instructions
